muslim voices

Welcome to the voices page! Featured here are Muslim run non-profits/organizations, as well as featured Muslim-made media/Muslims in mediia. While this page can't possibly contain all organizations, media, and Muslim inviduals out there, it does provide a very good place to start. Visitors are encouraged to explore and find something that catches their interest!
DISCLAIMER: This page does not claim or attempt to define what a Muslim is. With millions of Muslims around the world, Islam and being Muslim can be different for everybody depending on factors such as cultural background, socioeconomic class, etc. This page is only meant to bring attention to notable Muslim representation. It is not meant to condemn or criticize Muslims for how they choose to be Muslim.
